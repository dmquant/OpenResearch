3. Factors Driving Market Moves / 市场驱动因素

  • Federal Reserve Policy: Kept rates at 4.25-4.50%. Powell emphasized no rush to cut rates, pushing September rate-cut expectations below 50%.

    美联储政策： 维持利率在4.25-4.50%不变。鲍威尔强调“不急于降息”，导致9月降息预期降至50%以下。

  • Economic Data: Q2 GDP grew at 3.0%, beating expectations. However, underlying domestic demand grew at its slowest pace since 2022.

    经济数据： 第二季度GDP年化增长3.0%，超出预期。但国内潜在需求增速为2022年以来最慢。

  • Tariff Uncertainty: Trump's 50% tariff on copper products caused prices to plunge. An Aug. 1 deadline looms for tariffs on other partners.

    关税不确定性： 特朗普对铜产品征收50%关税导致铜价暴跌。对其他贸易伙伴的关税最后期限为8月1日。

  • Corporate Earnings: Mixed results. Strong after-hours reports from Microsoft (+8%) and Meta (+9%) boosted tech, while UPS (-10%) and Carrier (-11%) fell on weak guidance.

    公司财报： 结果好坏参半。微软（盘后+8%）和Meta（盘后+9%）的强劲财报提振了科技股，而UPS（-10%）和开利（-11%）则因指引疲软而下跌。
